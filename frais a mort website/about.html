WHAT IS "FRAIS À MORT"?

It's French slang for "Fresh to Death." It’s an attitude. A declaration. A commitment to style so definitive it’s lethal.

We are not a brand; we are a movement. Built on the ethos of exclusive, limited-quantity drops. Each piece is a statement—each collection, a moment in time.

No restocks. No repeats. Only what’s next.
